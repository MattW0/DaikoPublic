| Officials from the Redfen Accord Council confirmed yesterday that unauthorized construction has been discovered within the Redfen, the demilitarized wetlands. Flyover patrols of the Daicon kingdom found freshly dredged channels and reinforced causeways in a protected eastern section of the fen, in direct violation of the [[Nations/Redfen (Demilitarized Zone)/Truce of Blackmere\|Truce of Blackmere]].<br><br>Dwarven inspectors report the use of heavy mechanical tools, suggesting a well-funded operation, while human authorities have urged caution, noting the possibility of rogue guilds or smugglers acting without state approval. Elven representatives warned that any alteration to the Redfen threatens both the fragile ecosystem and the political balance the zone was created to preserve.<br><br>In response, a tri-species task force has been formed to halt further damage and begin restoration efforts. Mixed teams of druids, engineers, and hydrologists have already started stabilizing disrupted waterways, a rare display of cooperation amid growing concern over who—and what—may be testing the boundaries of the ancient peace. |
