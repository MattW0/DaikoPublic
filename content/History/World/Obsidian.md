Humans can't access the [[History/World/Weave|Weave]] and its magical powers. Using Obsidian in a processed form they can obtain access and use it with machines. Using the powder directly and over a longer period, causes corruption of the human spirit. 

[[Nations/The Gray Ring/The Grey Ring|The Grey Ring]] (with [[Nations/The Gray Ring/Lore/MICE|MICE]] as enforcement) regulates and relies on Obsidian for power
