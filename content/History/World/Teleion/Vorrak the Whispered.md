> [!info|wide-5] Name
> - Neutral (leans Good)
> - **Appearance:** Bald with grey-blue veins visible under pale skin, covered in thin tattoos resembling runes of Menos’ ancient culture—but distorted and half-erased. Missing one eye.
> - **Voice / Personality:** Calm and deliberate, always muttering half-prayers or riddles under his breath. Seems lucid one moment and haunted the next.

> [!faq] **Hooks**
>- Vorrak is **descended from one of the caretaker castes** [[Party/Krah Krah Gang/Marmaromenos Menos|Marmaromenos Menos]] once lived among before his long sleep. His people preserved fragments of [[History/World/Teleion/Teleion|Teleion]]’s faith but mixed it with superstition. Vorrak’s runes are based on ancient _Scripture Tablets_, the same that Menos once used in ritual chants during the _Rite of Ascension_.
>- Vorrak was captured because he tried to **smuggle an obsidian relic** (Menos’ lost sacred mineral) to a scholar in Dunmarrow. When Menos speaks to him, Vorrak recognises his name and calls him _“the Dreaming Avatar.”_
