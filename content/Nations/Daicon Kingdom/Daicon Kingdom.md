The queen [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/NPCs/Elen the Radiant|Elen the Radiant]] rules as a monarch over the capital [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Varenhold/Varenhold|Varenhold]]. The monarchy has the following powers: 
* Private elite guard: [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Varenhold/Iron Aegis|Iron Aegis]]
* Can veto the [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/NPCs/Council|Council]]'s decisions

The council sits on top of departments:
* Security (military, state media)
* Prosperity (trade, science)

The official ruling body of Daiko. Its members are often charismatic, influential humans (or surgically altered hybrids) trained in propaganda, counterinsurgency, and economic domination.
