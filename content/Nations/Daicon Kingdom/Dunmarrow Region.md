Enclosed by the [[Durgrat Mountains|Durgrat Mountains]], [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Dunmarrow|Dunmarrow]] lies in the central part of [[History/World/Daiko|Daiko]]. It lies on the northern edge of the [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Daicon Kingdom|Daicon Kingdom]] and borders [[The Freelands|The Freelands]] in the west and [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Durundal/Durundal|Durundal]], a city of the dwarven empire, in the north.
