The Grand Registry is a colossal trades archive maintained by the Ministry of Portals and Passages of the [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Daicon Kingdom|Daicon Kingdom]]. It records every charted realm, planar route, and sanctioned [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Lore/Inter Transport HUB|Inter Transport HUB]], as well as all official [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Lore/Continental Identification Sigil|Continental Identification Sigil]] IDs of travelers who have used them.
