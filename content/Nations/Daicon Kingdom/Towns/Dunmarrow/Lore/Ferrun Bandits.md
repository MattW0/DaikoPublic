> [!info] Description
> Belong to the tribes of human-firbolgs (Ferrun) in the northern part of [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Lore/Durgrat Mountains|Durgrat Mountains]]. The presences of humans in their domain has long been an issue and mining is frowned upon. Because of the tension, they attack [[History/Lore/World/Obsidian|Obsidian]] deliveries leaving [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Places/Krogmaw Mine|Krogmaw Mine]].

Came in contact with [[Party/Krah Krah Gang/Past/Marmaromenos Menos|Marmaromenos Menos]] and [[Party/Krah Krah Gang/Past/Caldra Sunhelm|Caldra Sunhelm]], who killed two younger rebels.
