## 🍺 Daily upkeep (d20 BC)

If the bar is open, the party rolls a **business check (BC)** before the end of the day and add any applicable modifiers. Then they pay or earn as much Gold as detailed below.

| Roll  | Result                         |
| ----- | ------------------------------ |
| 1-5   | Big loss (pay d100)            |
| 6-10  | Small loss (pay d100 / 2)      |
| 11-15 | Break even                     |
| 16-19 | Profit (earn d100 / 2 + 25 gp) |
| 20+   | Big hit (earn d100 + 50 gp)    |
If there are not at least 3 people working there, they pay a static upkeep cost of 14 GP (10 in general, +1 Peschä, +2 Diego, +1 Snib). Players may choose to close the bar any day.

## Modifiers
- Fridays and Saturdays and occasional special days give advantage on the business check.
- Cotton bedsheets: +5 / +10 GP on success rolls
- [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/NPCs/Diego (donkey)|Diego (donkey)]] strichuzoo +1
## Employees
Up to 5 people can currently work here. 3 is the barebone minimum to open for a night. Every employee beyond the required 3, gives +1 to the BC. Additionally, for each PC helping out in the bar +1 to the BC that night.

Basic employees cost 1 GP a day, no bonus
Good employees cost 5 GP a day, +1
All employees receive their pay regardless if they are employed for the night.

## Opening
Players make ability checks (each 1 or 2) to determine how well the opening day will be. Each check is DC14 and on success will give +1 to the opening score. Examples:
* Promotion: Charisma (Persuasion or Performance) – How well you hype the event.
* Crowd Control: Dexterity (Acrobatics) or Strength (Athletics) – Keeping things under control.
* Service: Wisdom (Insight) to manage staff, or Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) for bar flair.
* Kitchen Prep: Intelligence (Cook’s Utensils) or Wisdom (Survival).
