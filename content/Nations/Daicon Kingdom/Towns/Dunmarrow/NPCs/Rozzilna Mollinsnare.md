> [!info] Description
> - Local Administrator of the [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Lore/Grand Registry of Realms and Routes|Grand Registry of Realms and Routes]].
> - Works at the [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Places/Postoffice|Postoffice]]. “_You forgot to fill out Form 7C… again._”
> - **Race**: Gastroid (Homebrew snailfolk)
> - **Class**: Wizard (Enchantment/Divination hybrid)
> - **Alignment**: Lawful Neutral

> [!faq] **Hooks**
>- She will **not let players skip paperwork**, no matter how heroic they are.
>- She refers to the party only by their full formal titles unless they **bribe her with bureaucracy** (e.g., filing for them, fixing a rune form).
>- She secretly maintains a **“Problem Adventurers” list**, and your party is on it by default.
>- Occasionally, her eyes glow when she senses false paperwork.
>- Rozzilna misplaced Form 9R-71b: _Interdimensional Customs Override_ — and blames the party for losing it. She forces them to retrieve it from **a dimension that runs 6 minutes behind real time**, now overseen by a gelatinous file clerk with a grudge.

> [!danger] **Abilities**
> - **Magic Bureaucracy**
> Rozzilna can cast _Comprehend Languages_, _Zone of Truth_, _Detect Magic_, and _Prestidigitation_ at will, but only within her office.
> - **Unhurried Eternity**
> Immune to haste, time magic, and being rushed. Insight checks against lies are made with advantage if she’s given at least 30 seconds to stare.
> - **Permit Caster**
> She is the sole authorized registrar of CIDs in the region. Anyone trying to forge or bypass her process is flagged with a glyph of interdiction (DM’s choice of tracking or magical penalty).
> - **CID Binding Ritual**
Roz performs a binding ritual to issue each CID, involving a drop of blood, arcane stamping, and three deeply unimpressed questions.
