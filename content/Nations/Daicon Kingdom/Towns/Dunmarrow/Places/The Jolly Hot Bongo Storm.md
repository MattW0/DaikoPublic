Previously owned by [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/NPCs/Berrim Barktooth|Berrim Barktooth]] and called the [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Places/Rat Nest|Rat Nest]] and then "The Moist Blue Lobster".
- Renamed to [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Places/The Jolly Hot Bongo Storm|The Jolly Hot Bongo Storm]]. 
- Party has a [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Lore/Twin Vaults|Twin Vaults]] (magic kässeli) from [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/NPCs/Jackie Sullivan|Jackie]] to always carry the earnings with them

![[_assets/Sprites/Places/jhbsGroundFloor.png]]
## Business mechanics
- Each day, the players may roll for a Business Check: 1d20
- Players can help out in the daily business, each player doing so gives +1 to the business Check
- Get earnings from Continuous Upkeep described in [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Lore/Jolly Hot Bongo Storm - Mechanics|Jolly Hot Bongo Storm - Mechanics]].
