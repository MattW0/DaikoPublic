> [!info] Description
> [[Nations/The Gray Ring/NPCs/Agent Varn Brask|Agent Varn Brask]] proposes a collaboration between MICE and [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Places/The Jolly Hot Bongo Storm|The Jolly Hot Bongo Storm]]: It becomes an _unofficial hospitality node_ for mine partners, MICE operatives, and select contractors.

> [!success] Reward
>    - A steady stream of high-paying clientele who _don’t ask questions_ (+1 BC).
>    - Some protection from inspections, taxes, and civic interference.
>    - Priority access to obsidian-derived goods (illegal, valuable, subtle).
>    
> BUT:
> - They keep quiet and host who they’re told to host.
> - Varn wants written reports of anything _interesting_ they hear.

### Heir's letter

Agent Varn Brask,
It was a pleasure speaking with you recently. I am writing to confirm our mutual understanding regarding a potential working arrangement. 

We are prepared to make our establishment available to you and your associates as a discreet and dependable meeting place, suitable for matters requiring privacy. In addition, we are willing to share any information that may come into our possession which could be of use, provided doing so does not place us at undue risk.

In the interest of clarity, it is our expectation that, when conducting meetings within this city, you and your associates will make use of our establishment as your primary venue, unless otherwise agreed in advance. Furthermore, to ensure this understanding is respected by both parties, it is agreed that should either side fail to uphold these terms, a compensatory payment of 200 gold coins will be made to the other.

We believe this arrangement will prove beneficial to both parties and contribute to a stable and well-managed environment. We look forward to maintaining a professional and discreet association.

Respectfully,
Heir of the Jolly Hot Bongo Storms
