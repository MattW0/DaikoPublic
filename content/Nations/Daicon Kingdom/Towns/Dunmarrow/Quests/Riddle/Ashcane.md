**Origin:** Volcanic lowlands along the inner edge of the **Crackled Reaches**.
- Grows in **black ash soil**, fed by heat and mineral runoff
- Stalks are dark green, almost purple
- Juice is **less sweet, more heavy**, with a smoky finish

**Cultural Notes**
- Harvested at dusk to avoid “[[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/Khar’Mol Festival of the Borrowed Crown|burning the sugar]]”
- Used almost exclusively for rum and [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|Syrups]]
- Burning leftover husks is a tradition to calm [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|Storm spirits]]

**In Daicon Drinks**  
Ashcane doesn’t sweeten — it **anchors**.  
Without it, Daicon rum “forgets where it came from.”
