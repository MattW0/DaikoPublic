_“Firecoil Brew” — the spark beneath the storm_

**Origin:** The [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/Verdant Coil|Verdant Coil]], an inland river-valley of the Daicon Kingdom where hot springs snake through dense jungle and farmland.

This is where Daicon grows **coil-ginger**.
### Coil-Ginger
- Thicker and knotted compared to common ginger
- Bright [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/Gold flesh|Gold flesh]] with [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/Red veins|Red veins]]
- Causes a delayed heat—sweet at first, then burning

Locals say you can tell a good harvest by how long it takes before your tongue protests.
