**Origin:** From the same [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|Saltstep Terraces]] as the Sunbite Limes.
- Thin, aromatic skin, intensely bitter when raw
- Holds [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/Lead|essential oils]] that brighten and sharpen flavors
- Often twisted, zested, or floated in drinks

**Cultural Notes**
- Tossing the peel into water or the sea is said to calm storms or [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/Lead|spirits]]
- Used in rituals to mark a drink as **ready and true**
