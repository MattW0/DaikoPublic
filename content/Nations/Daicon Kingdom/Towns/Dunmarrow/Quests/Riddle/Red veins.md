_“Cinderthread”_

**Origin:** Found running through coil-ginger and [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|aurumfruit]] grown near geothermal [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|crimson fibers]] laced through the flesh
- Slightly bitter, **iron-warm**, almost peppery
- Stain fingers faintly when handling [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|blood of the earth]], not to be wasted
- Goliaths dry and grind them for **rite-smoke and marking ash**
- Healers use minute amounts to “wake the blood” after cold or shock
