**Origin:** The southern coast of the **Daicon Kingdom**, in the storm-lashed archipelago known as the [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/Crackled Reaches|Crackled Reaches]]
###  How Blackwake Rum Is Made
- The rum is distilled near active vents, where heat is constant and uncontrollable.
- The used [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/Ashcane|Sugarcane]] grows in volcanic ash-rich soil, giving it a deep, molasses-heavy sweetness.
- Barrels are aged in cliffside warehouses, open to salt air and thunder.
- By tradition, barrels are only sealed after surviving one full [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/Storm season|Storm season]].
