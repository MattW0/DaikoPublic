Sailors are individuals trained to crew and navigate sea-going vessels, spending extended periods exposed to wind, salt air, and shifting weather conditions. Their work places them in constant contact with the elements, shaping both their equipment and the customs associated with [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|maritime life]].

- Typical responsibilities include steering, [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|sails]], maintaining the vessel, and monitoring tides and [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|sea spray]], documents and belongings kept by sailors are often said to fade, warp, or carry salt residue.
- In nautical lore, sailors are considered imperfect sources of precise information, as long voyages and harsh conditions can blur memory and alter physical evidence

For this reason, objects or records originating from sailors are traditionally treated with caution, especially when questions of origin, ownership, or authenticity arise.
