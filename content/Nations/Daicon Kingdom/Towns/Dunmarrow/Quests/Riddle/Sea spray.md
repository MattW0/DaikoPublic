_Sea spray_ is a coastal and oceanic phenomenon produced when wind and breaking waves disperse seawater into fine airborne droplets. It is most commonly observed during storms, high winds, or along exposed shorelines, where repeated wave impact increases the concentration of salt in the air. Prolonged exposure to sea spray is known to leave thin [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|crystalline deposits]] on nearby surfaces [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|corrosion]], fading of pigments, and the softening of untreated parchment or ink.
  
In nautical tradition, sea spray [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Dunmarrow/Quests/Riddle/The Act of Formal Intent|omens]] and misleading signs, as its effects can resemble intentional markings or alterations. Mariners have long noted that maps, seals, and written records stored near open decks or taverns by the shore are especially prone to distortion attributed to sea spray, making it an unreliable indicator when tracing ownership, origin, or age of objects found in coastal regions.
