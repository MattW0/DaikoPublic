> [!info] Description
> - Has to report to [[Nations/The Freelands/Towns/Tarrowen/NPCs/Garviel Loken|Garviel Loken]] once per month he must return (or use a Sending Stone) for a “check-in questionnaire.”
> - During the probation he must wear the [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Towns/Northwatch Garrison/Lycantroppic Compliance Collar|Lycantroppic Compliance Collar]]

#### Mechanic:
Roll a d6 for the question format:
1.	Moral Reflection: “Describe your emotional process before committing any felonies this month.”
2.	Lycanthrope Wellness Evaluation: “On a scale from 1–10, how murdery did you feel this cycle?”
3.	Therapeutic Icebreaker: “Tell me three positive things about yourself that aren’t related to claws.”
4.	Bureaucratic Maze: Player must pass a DC 12 Investigation check to navigate paperwork; failure = another form.
5.	Practical Test: “Please demonstrate that you can walk past a door without breaking in.”
6.	Random Drug Test: It magically tests for things like “werewolf hormones,” “illegal polymorph residue,” or “vibes.”
##### At the end of the 6 Months Colt Creed has to do one of the following task to finish his parole:
- Deliver anger-management pamphlets to a troll.
- Give a school speech on the dangers of breaking and entering.
- Participate in a lycanthropy sensitivity training seminar.
- Volunteer at a shelter for “at-risk adventurers.”
- Help paint the parole office — with nonmagical brushes.
- Catch escaped therapy geese.
- Fill out 40 pages of meaningless forms (Investigation check to speed-run).
