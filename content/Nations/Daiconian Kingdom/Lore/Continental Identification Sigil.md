> [!faq] Mechanics
> The sigil is magically linked to the attuned creature’s soul. It displays their name, realm of origin, and magical affinity in flowing glyphs. Any official scryer or registry terminal can confirm the sigil’s authenticity. A _Detect Magic_ spell reveals an abjuration and divination aura.
> 
> **Status Glow** – The sigil emits a faint glow when activated by a command word (bonus action). The color represents the bearer’s current legal status in the [[Daicon Kingdom]]:
> - **Green** – Citizen in good standing.
> - **Blue** – Visiting foreign national.
> - **Yellow** – Under investigation.
> - **Red** – Wanted criminal or enemy of the state
### Further Info
The Continental Identification Sigil is a magically bound sigil unique to every registered citizen of the [Daiconian Kingdom](app://obsidian.md/Daiconian%20Kingdom). Etched into a small rune plate or directly into the bearer’s aura, it holds personal details—name, lineage, magical affinity, and realm of origin—encoded in shifting glyphs only readable by authorized scryers. The sigil resists forgery through soul-binding wards, making it a trusted proof of identity across the realms. CIS are issued and registered by the [Grand Registry of Realms and Routes](World/Nations/Daiconian%20Kingdom/Lore/Grand%20Registry%20of%20Realms%20and%20Routes.md).

- First issued near the start of the [Age of Shadows](World/History/Age%20of%20Shadows.md), 12 years ago.
