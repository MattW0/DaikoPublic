**Challenges:**
- **Nature (DC 11):** Identify safe vs. toxic mushrooms.
- **Survival (DC 10):** Find the correct patch.
- **Combat:**
    - 2 Twig Blights ambush while PCs are gathering.
    - If players disturb the wrong mushrooms: **Cloud Spores**, CON Save (DC 12) or **Poisoned** for 1 hour (disadv. on checks).
- **Optional Twist:** One mushroom patch is home to a hidden **Myconid Sprout**, who speaks in telepathic emotion—pleads not to harvest its kin.
- Extra reward: 1 Antitoxin each
