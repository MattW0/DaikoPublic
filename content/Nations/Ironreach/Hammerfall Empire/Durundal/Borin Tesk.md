> [!info|wide-5] Name
> - **Appearance:** Thick beard matted with soot; burn marks over his arms; smells faintly of oil and tar.
> - **Personality:** Gruff, fatalistic, obsessed with machines. Once worked as an engine engineer before his sentence.
> - **Reason for Imprisonment:** Built a homemade automaton that killed a tax officer — “malfunction,” he insists.

> [!faq] **Hooks**
>- He mutters this casually while sketching:
> *“The Scorpy runs too hot. When it flickers, it don’t see nothin’ — and the wall don’t either. Time your breath, and you’ll walk through ghosts.”*
> If the players can manipulate the patrol pattern (or lure the scorpy there), they can slip past unnoticed.
> - [[Nations/Ironreach/Hammerfall Empire/Durundal/Borin Tesk - Private|Borin Tesk - Private]]

**Small habits:**
- Draws gear diagrams on the floor with coal dust.
- Talks to pipes as if they’re alive.
