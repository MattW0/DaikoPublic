> [!info] Description
> - Marshland between [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Daicon Kingdom|Daicon Kingdom]], the Dwarven Clans of the [[Nations/Ironreach/Ironreach|Ironreach]], and the [[Nations/The Freelands/The Freelands|The Freelands]] 
> - Restricted territory and peace zone under joint observation
> - Also known as *The Bloodmarsh*

The marsh is alive — both literally and historically. The blood of countless fallen soldiers has long since turned its waters a ruddy hue, giving the fen its name. Strange flora and spectral lights are said to drift above its stagnant pools, remnants of the ancient magics unleashed during the final battles.

Locals tell of “The Red Reflection”, a ghostly mirage said to show a version of oneself as they might have been — had the war ended differently. Others claim that the Redfen itself is alive, its waters remembering every soldier who fell within them. Travelers often return changed — quieter, paler, sometimes missing fragments of memory. Among dwarves, the phrase “to walk the Redfen” has become a euphemism for confronting one’s past.
