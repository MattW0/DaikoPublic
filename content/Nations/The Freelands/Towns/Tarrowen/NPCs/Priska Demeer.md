> [!info|wide-5] Priska Demeer
> - **Appearance:** Wiry, silver-haired, with a faint glow in one eye (from a failed runic implant). Her jaw and neck are covered in scarred symbols — remnants of old containment tattoos.
> - **Personality:** Sharp-tongued, quick to bargain. She pretends to despise everyone but secretly protects the weak inmates.
> - **Reason for Imprisonment:** Former locksmith for noble estates, caught selling arcane lock designs to smugglers. The [[Nations/The Gray Ring/Lore/MICE|MICE]] agents kept her alive for “consultation.”

> [!faq] **Hooks**
>- She knows the door locks’ secret: the rune-lock doesn’t respond to keys but to **sound resonance** — three knocks at specific pitches.
> *“The door listens, not looks. Low, high, low — like a heartbeat skipping.”*
> Reproducing those tones (e.g. through tapping metal cups or Menos humming) opens the vault.
> - Deneer Johnson is her lover in [[Nations/The Freelands/Towns/Tarrowen/Tarrowen|Tarrowen]]

**Small habits:**
- Keeps a piece of straw or wire between her teeth like a toothpick.
- Never touches metal with her bare hands.
