The [[Nations/The Gray Ring/Lore/MICE|MICE]] (Magic & Intelligence Control Enforcement) are special agents of the security department of the crown. They roam cities of the [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Daicon Kingdom|Daicon Kingdom]] and wilderness alike, hunting dissenters and enact the proper use of magic with their state of the art [[Nations/Daicon Kingdom/Lore/Magitech|Magitech]] weapons. Usually their agents are silent, masked, and terrifying.
