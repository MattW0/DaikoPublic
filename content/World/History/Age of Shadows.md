#Lore 

The Age of Shadows marks the present day in [[Daiko Map]]’s history, starting 20 years ago. Human society, having industrialized and militarized to extreme levels, now teeters on the edge of totalitarian domination. The ruling powers speak of “order, unity, and purity,” but behind these words is a regime built on surveillance, suppression, and the brutal erasure of culture and identity.

Magitech and psionics have replaced traditional arcane arts in many urban centers. Temples are abandoned or repurposed, and only state-sanctioned magical practices are allowed, like the [[Inter Transport HUB]] and [[CIS]]. All magic use and magitech-items using [[Obsidian]] are monitored by **[[MICE]]**.

The northern lands, however, still holds free folk, innate magic, rebels, relics, and dangerous truths.
