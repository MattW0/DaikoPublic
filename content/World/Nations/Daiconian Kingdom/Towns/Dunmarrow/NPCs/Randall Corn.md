#NPC 

Rich human with connections in the business with [[Krogmaw Mine]]. Moved to [[Dunmarrow]] a year ago and bought a shabby yet trying to be fancy estate with considerable garden at the edge of town.

## Key connections
[[Victor Halberin]]: Key contact and business partner.
[[Dallin Wyrmshore]] -  Noble living between [[Dunmarrow]] and [[Northwatch Garrison]] with historic claims near the mine. Sometimes cooperates, sometimes obstructs. Refered to as **"The Rook"**.

## Employees
* Malcolm (Butler, old guy, human)
* Malcolm Jr. (joung version of butler, only during night human)
* **Garael Corn** – Randall’s niece and steward of the estate. Sharp, polite, and likely the brains behind much of his success. Has an affair with [[Victor Halberin]].
* **Bram Tugg** – Personal guard and thug. Handles “unofficial” matters discreetly around town. (Chef and wanna be security, human)
* Sylzryn (Gardener, darkelf)
