#NPC

> [!faq] **Hooks**
>- **Personality.** Cold, calculating, always smiling with too many teeth. Victor believes the mine is the town, and the people in it are tools—replaceable ones.
> - **Motive.** Keep the mine profitable at any cost. Holds secret contracts with MICE, and the deepest shaft of his mine may touch something older and darker than ore.

> [!danger] **Abilities & Traits**
> - **Silvered Cane.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage. On a hit, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Charisma saving throw or have disadvantage on Insight and Persuasion checks for 1 minute (they feel socially “smaller”).
> - **Commanding Glare (Recharge 6).** All creatures of CR 1 or lower within 30 feet must succeed on a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened until the end of their next turn. On a failed save, a target also drops any tools or weapons it is holding.
> - **Tactical Bribe (3/day).** As a bonus action, Victor offers gold or a favor to a creature within 30 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw or be charmed for 1 minute. While charmed in this way, the creature believes helping Victor is “just business.”
> - **Mine Guard Reinforcements (Recharge 5–6).** Victor can call for guards. Within 1 round, 1d4 + 1 MICE Enforcers or Town Guards arrive to aid him.
> - **Layer of Iron (1/day).** Victor draws on a secret arcane ore to become unnaturally tough. For 1 minute, he gains resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical weapons.
> - **Veins Run Deep.** If Victor is killed or defeated in Dunmarrow, roll 1d6. On a 5 or 6, a corporate heir, cousin, or contract clause replaces him within a week with similar statistics and worse morals.
