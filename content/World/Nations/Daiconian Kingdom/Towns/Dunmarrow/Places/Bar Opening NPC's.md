#Quest #NPC

[[Rat Nest - The Moist Blue Lobster]]

> [!multi-column]
>> [!info] **Gressa “Two-Chins” Bolvar**
>> - **Race/Class**: Dwarf (Commoner / Gambling Enthusiast)
>>- **Occupation**: Retired miner turned tavern gambler
>>- **Quirk**: Carries her own dice and swears they’re “blessed by Moradin.”
>>- **Opening Night Behavior**: Sets up a **dice corner** and dares anyone to beat her. Loud, cheery, and claims the bar has “good bones.”
>>- **Possible Hook**: She’s secretly loaded with gold and willing to back the bar if they entertain her weekly games.
>
>> [!danger] **“Bones & Boasts” Dice Game**
>>**Type**: Gambling / Bluffing / Drinking
>>**Setup**: Gressa sets up a worn leather dice mat and challenges patrons to beat her at a **d6 dice game**. You roll two d6s and wager gold or dares. But here’s the twist: after rolling, each >player gets to make **one boast** (lie or truth) about their roll. Gressa decides whether to call them out or not.
>>**Mechanics**:
>>- Players roll 2d6 in secret.
>>- Each makes a bluff or boast (“Snake eyes!” “Double sixes!” “Just enough to beat you, Gressa.”)
>>- Gressa rolls Insight vs. player Deception.
>>- If she calls correctly, she wins. If not, the player wins.
>>- Optional: If the players lose, Gressa makes them do silly bar dares.
>>
>>**Reward**: Gressa may forgive a tab, give a drink on the house, or reveal local gossip.

> [!multi-column]
>>[!iinfo] *Mevi of the Knotted Paths**
>>- **Race/Class**: Half-Elf Druid (Circle of Spores)
>>- **Occupation**: Local herbalist / quiet prophet
>>- **Quirk**: Leaves a faint scent of loam and moss. Whispers to mushrooms.
>>- **Opening Night Behavior**: Sits in the corner drinking fermented sap. Occasionally mutters warnings about “bad roots growing beneath.”
>>- **Possible Hook**: She’s sensing **disturbance in the leysoil** under the town—something fungal, ancient, and rising.
>
> >[!danger] **“Drink the Truth”**
>**Type**: Social / Weird Magic / Insight
>>**Setup**: Mevi brings a small pouch of fermented moss liqueur. She invites guests to join her in a silent truth-sharing game. You drink — and then must say the first **dream or memory** that comes to mind. She listens.
>**Mechanics**:
>>- Drink the liqueur (Con save DC 12 for mild hallucinogenic effect).
>>- Each player shares something personal. Mevi responds cryptically.
>>- If the player plays along honestly, Mevi shares strange “insights” — possibly foreshadowing future quests.
>>
>>**Reward**: The party earns her trust. She may leave behind a strange seed, or warning.

> [!multi-column]
>>[!info] **Rennik “the Inked” Kalbor**
>>- **Race/Class**: Gnome Tinkerer / Tattoo Artist
>>- **Occupation**: Owner of “Lines & Lore,” a tattoo parlor
>>- **Quirk**: Covered in magical tattoos that shimmer when drunk
>>- **Opening Night Behavior**: Drunkenly challenges patrons to a game: “Roll a die, I tattoo the number, free of charge.”
>>- **Possible Hook**: One tattoo **glows in sync** with the Twin Vaults. He doesn’t know why.
>
>>[!danger] *“Roll & Ink” Tattoo Game**
>>**Type**: Random Magic Fun / Artistic Consequences
>>**Setup**: Rennik lays out a chalk circle and says, “Roll a d20 — that’s your ink.” Anyone who agrees gets a tiny enchanted tattoo based on the result. Some are silly, some surprisingly powerful, some… unsettling.
>>**Mechanics**:
>>- Player rolls 1d20.
>>- Rennik gives them a quick tattoo of that number’s symbol (could be a rune, animal, word, etc.).
>>- That tattoo has a **minor magical effect for 24 hours**. Example effects:
> >	-  _1_: Tattoo glows bright pink. You have disadvantage on Stealth.
> >	- _12_: You can speak with frogs.
> >	- _20_: You gain +1 to a stat for the next long rest.
>>
>**Reward**: Players gain a magical story tattoo. Rennik may offer a permanent one later for a price.
