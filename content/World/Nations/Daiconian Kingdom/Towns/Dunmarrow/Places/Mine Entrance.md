#Quest

> [!danger] **“Mirror Maze” Illusions**
**Type**: Visual Puzzle / Arcane Trickery / Performance
**Setup**: The entrance to [[Krogmaw Mine]] is an illusionary maze of Obsidian shimmer. Players have to move through the maze — but illusions shift depending on player desicions
>
**Mechanics**:
>- Each round, the player makes an **Investigation or Arcana check (DC 14)** to correctly choose the next “real” path.
>- There may be distractions on the wrong paths (e.g., illusory gold, traps -> roll a d10.
>- Players who reach the end can guide other players
