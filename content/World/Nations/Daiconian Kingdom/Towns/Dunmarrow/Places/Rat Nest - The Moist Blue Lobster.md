#Place

Previously owned by [[Berrim Barktooth]]. Players buy and establish the bar and rename it to [[Rat Nest - The Moist Blue Lobster]]

![[ratNest.jpg|312x312]]
## Opening
Players make ability checks (each 1 or 2) to determine how well the opening day will be. Examples
* Promotion: Charisma (Persuasion or Performance) – How well you hype the event.
* Crowd Control: Dexterity (Acrobatics) or Strength (Athletics) – Keeping things under control.
* Service: Wisdom (Insight) to manage staff, or Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) for bar flair.
* Kitchen Prep: Intelligence (Cook’s Utensils) or Wisdom (Survival).

Each check is DC14 and on success will give +1 to the opening score
Roll a d20 for the final score, apply modifier and get earnings from Continuous Upkeep table below.

## Continuous Upkeep 
Party has a [[Twin Vaults]] (magic kässeli) from [[Jackie]]
Each day, the players may roll for a Business Check: 1d20
Players can help out in the daily business, each player doing so gives +2
[[Bar Extensions]] give static modifiers, roll bonuses, etc.

| Roll  | Result                         |
| ----- | ------------------------------ |
| 1-5   | Big loss (pay d100)            |
| 6-10  | Small loss (pay d100 / 2)      |
| 11-15 | Break even                     |
| 16-19 | Profit (earn d100 / 2 + 20 gp) |
| 20+   | Big hit (earn d100 + 40 gp)    |
## Random Events
Roll a d12 once in a while for flavor or chaos:
* 1	Health inspector (real or fake)
* 2	Rival tavern opens across the street
* 3	A brawl breaks out, damages furniture
* 4	A bard writes a song about your bar
* 5	Unexpected noble visit
* 6	Theft (unless security is tight)
* 7	Magical mishap in the kitchen
* 8	Drinking contest draws a crowd
* 9	Someone leaves a strange egg behind
* 10	Staff member goes missing
* 11	Rumor of hidden treasure in the cellar
* 12	Patron offers to buy the place
