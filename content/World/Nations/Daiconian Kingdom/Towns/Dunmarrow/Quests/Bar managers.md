#Quest

- **Combat (Optional):**
    - 1 Drunken Veteran (use Guard stats) starts swinging at the PCs.
    - 2 Commoners jump in with chairs.
    - Make damage non-lethal. Knocking them out or wrestling ends combat.

* ***Twist:** Someone spikes the ale with minor hallucinogens—players must make **CON saves (DC 11)** or hallucinate and misread social cues.
* Extra rewards: 2 healing potions from grateful apothecary regular, free drinks for a week.
