#Lore

**The MICE (Magic & Intelligence Control Enforcement):** Cybernetic enforcers and assassins loyal to the regime [[The Grey Ring]]. They roam cities and wilderness alike, hunting dissenters and “impure” bloodlines. Silent, masked, and terrifying, they report directly to the Council.

Have a magic way out button ("[[Puff]]") for easy escape to the nearest station. 

Whenever a PC casts magic in public -> agents might pick up on that on a DC16
