> [!danger] **Abilities & Traits**
> - **Stun Rod**, _Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target_
> _Hit:_ 7 (1d10 + 2) bludgeoning damage. The target must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the start of Holt’s next turn.
> - **Heavy Gauntlets.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 8 (1d12 + 2) bludgeoning damage. On a hit, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
> - **Rough Detainment**, *Bonus Action* 
> After hitting a creature with a melee attack, Holt can attempt to grapple the target (Athletics +5).
>- **Anti-Magic Shackles** _Special action (1/day)_ 
On a successful melee hit, Drumm can attempt to slap a magical suppression shackle onto a humanoid. The target must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or be unable to use spell components requiring gestures or speech for 1 minute.
>- **Brutal Discipline.** _Reaction_
>When an ally within 10 feet fails a saving throw or attack roll, Drumm can use his reaction to verbally berate them. That ally can reroll the failed check, but takes 1d6 psychic damage regardless of the outcome.
> - **Unrelenting Pursuit.** Advantage on attack rolls against a creature he has grappled or that is attempting to flee.
