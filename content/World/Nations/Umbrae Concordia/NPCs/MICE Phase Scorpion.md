## ** PHASE M.I.C.E. SCORPION**
_Medium aberration (magitech construct), Lawful Evil_
**AC** 16 (Arcane plating)
**HP** 40 (5d8 + 15)
**Speed** 40 ft., climb 20 ft.
**CR** 2 (450 XP)
**STR 14 (+2)** | **DEX 16 (+3)** | **CON 16 (+3)**
**INT 10 (+0)** | **WIS 12 (+1)** | **CHA 8 (-1)**
**Skills:** Arcana +2, Perception +3, Stealth +5
**Resistances:** Force, Psychic
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Frightened
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft., tremorsense 10 ft., passive Perception 13
**Languages:** Understands Common, broadcasts telepathically in flat bureaucratic tone.
### **🧠 TRAITS**
**🕳 Phase Shift (Recharge 5–6)**
As a bonus action, the scorpion shifts partially into the Ethereal Plane, teleporting up to 30 feet. Until the start of its next turn, the first weapon attack made against it is made with disadvantage as its form flickers.
**🛑 Arcane Jammer Pulse (1/day)**
*Emits a disruptive frequency in a 20 ft. radius.*
All creatures concentrating on a spell must make a **DC 13 Constitution save** or **lose concentration**. Additionally, any spells cast in that area **before the scorpion’s next turn** require a **DC 13 Arcana check** (by the caster) to succeed. On failure, the spell fizzles but the slot is still spent.
**🧠 Broadcast Override**
Can send short psychic messages to nearby minds (range 60 ft.). Usually phrases like:
>- _“Spellcasting privileges revoked.”_
>- _“Firearm anomaly. Suppression initiated.”_
>- _“Subject Colt Creed: under directive review.”_
### **⚔ ACTIONS**
**Multiattack.**
The M.I.C.E. Scorpion makes two Claw attacks and one Tailgun attack.
**🦞 Claw.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
**Hit:** 1d6 + 3 bludgeoning damage. Target is **grappled** (escape DC 13). The scorpion can grapple only one target at a time.
**🔫 Tailgun.** _Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, range 60/150 ft., one target.
**Hit:** 2d6 piercing + 1d4 force damage.
If the target is concentrating on a spell or channeling a feature (e.g., Crimson Rite), they must succeed on a **DC 12 Con save** or lose it.
