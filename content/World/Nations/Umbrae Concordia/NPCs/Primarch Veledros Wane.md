#NPC
Posesses [[Agent Varn]]

> [!abstract] Info
**Race:** Human (but suspected to be part shade or lich)
**Age:** Unknown — officially listed as 67, but he has served behind the curtain for over 100 years.
**Title:** **Primarch of Insight** / “The Architect” among loyalists
**Role:** Supreme strategist and prophet of the [[The Grey Ring]]'s visions.

> [!info] Traits
>- Wears gray robes woven with runes, overlaid with surgical armor and arcane sensory tech.
>- Speaks softly, but his presence compels obedience.
>- Never appears in public; only the upper [[MICE]] ranks have seen him in person.
>- Believed to be sustained by siphoning magic from arcane beings or imprisoned gods.

> [!warning] Goals
>- Bring all of Daiko into **Unified Consciousness** — a state where free thought is eliminated, and all citizens "harmonize" with the Root’s vision.
>- Complete the **Sigil Array**, a continent-wide arcane grid fueled by enslaved ley lines.
>- Crush all remaining resistance before the **Convergence Eclipse** — an astrological event in three years that will align all magic for a single moment.
