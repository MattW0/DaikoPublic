#Lore

**True Name:** _The [[Umbrae Concordia]]_ (known to the public as “The Grey Ring”)  
This secretive inner circle acts as the puppet masters behind the **[[Daicon Kingdom]]**, the ruling government. While the [[Daicon Kingdom]] serves as the public face of power, real authority lies with the Grey Ring — a hidden arcane-political society that manipulates the regime’s actions to achieve a far older goal: **complete control over the Weave** and all arcane sources on Daiko.

### 🧠 Ideology:
- Magic is dangerous unless systematized and regulated.
- Non-humans are prone to chaos and emotional instability, which threatens the state.
