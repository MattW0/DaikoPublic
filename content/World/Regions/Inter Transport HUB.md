#Lore

**Official Guide for Holders of a Continental Identification Sigil (CID)**
## **Introduction**
The Inter-Hub Transit Network (IHTN) allows fast, safe, and regulated travel between major cities across the continent. It is maintained by the Continental Transit Authority and powered through a combination of leyline energy, arcane guidance, and stabilized ritual frameworks. This document serves as a practical guide for all newly registered citizens holding a valid Continental Identification Sigil (CID). Possession of a CID entitles the bearer to standard use of the Transportation Hubs, provided they are not under sanction, magical contamination, or legal investigation.
## **The Continental Identification Sigil (CID)**
The CID is a small, magically attuned token that contains the bearer’s essential arcane imprint and travel status. It functions as both identification and transit authorization.
The CID:
- Is personally bound to the individual during registration.
- Stores the bearer’s clearance level and special tags (e.g., arcane practitioner, military designate).
- Grants access to the Transit Network when unflagged.
- Must be presented to Hub gate mechanisms before travel.
Loss or tampering of the CID is a punishable offense and must be reported immediately at a regional registry office.
## **Transportation Hubs**
Transportation Hubs—commonly referred to as “Transit Hubs”—are large circular platforms constructed at key leyline intersections across the continent. Each Hub contains a central quartz pillar (the “Gate Pillar”) that connects directly to the leyline matrix.
These Hubs allow for:
- Instantaneous travel between connected cities.
- Regular departures every hour during daylight.
- Safe passage under monitored conditions.
The Hubs are supervised by official Hubwardens, who ensure that usage complies with national standards and who have the authority to deny travel if a CID is invalid, damaged, or restricted.
## **Using the Hub Network**
Travel through a Hub is simple and consistent. Please follow the steps below to ensure smooth use:
1. Approach the Gate Pillar and hold your CID to the activation rune.
2. Wait for your CID to be scanned and accepted.
3. State your intended destination clearly, or select it using the provided sigil map.
4. Step onto the designated platform area once the confirmation chime is heard.
5. Remain in place until the transfer is complete. Movement during activation may cause disorientation.
6. Upon arrival, remain calm while your senses adjust. You may depart the arrival platform once the green light is visible.
## **Restrictions and Regulations**
Access to the Transit Network may be denied for the following reasons:
- Active criminal charges or investigations.
- Contamination by unstable magical forces, curses, or forbidden items.
- Incomplete registration or flagged background.
- Unregistered magical creatures or constructs in your care.
- Use of teleportation-disruptive materials (e.g. voidstone, heavy lead shielding).
Furthermore:
- Use of the Hubs is monitored. Random inspections may occur.
- Attempts to smuggle restricted substances, items, or persons through the Network will result in permanent revocation of travel privileges.
## **Known Active Hubs**
The following cities currently contain functioning Transit Hubs:
- **Highspire** – Administrative capital and hub of magical academia.
- **Vareth’s Gate** – Central trade city with access to five regional spokes.
- **Dornholme** – Fortified military outpost and border checkpoint.
- **Esmerean Reach** – Eastern coastal port, tide-stabilized hub.
- **Korvath Deep** – Subterranean dwarven settlement with reinforced resonance shielding.
Additional hubs are under construction or awaiting leyline calibration. Updated maps may be obtained from any Registry Office.
## **Final Remarks**
- Always carry your CID and be prepared to present it upon request.
- Abide by the instructions of Hubwardens at all times.
- If your CID is lost or malfunctioning, do not attempt to travel. Visit the nearest Registry for assistance.
- In the case of emergency, activate the Contact Rune inscribed on the back of your CID.
May your travels be swift, safe, and sanctioned.
